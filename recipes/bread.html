Bread

Description

This is probably as simple as you can get. Rustic. Easy. Cottagecore. This recipe shouldn't take you more than 20 minutes of work.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Instant yeast
  • Salt
  • Warm water
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Make sure you leave the butter out so that it becomes soft. Rub this butter on the inside of a Pyrex bowl.
  2. Stir together the flour, salt, and yeast in a separate bowl.
  3. Add warm water and stir. It should bubble.
  4. Let the bread rise for an hour in a warm place.
  5. Transfer the dough to a buttered bowl and bake on 450 for 20 minutes.
