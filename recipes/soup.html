Soup

Description

This soup looks exactly like the soup in the igloo from Animal Crossing for GameCube.

Ingredients

  • Bouillon
  • Carrots
  • Potato
  • Greek yogurt
  • Green onion
  • Dill

Steps

  1. Chop the carrots and potatoes.
  2. Fry the vegetables in some olive oil until softened.
  3. Add hot water and a bouillon cube.
  4. Let cook, add dill at last moment.
  5. Top with some Greek yogurt.
