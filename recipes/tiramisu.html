Tiramisu

Description

The world's most underrated dessert.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Coffee
  • Mascarpone
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Whip the mascarpone and sugar together until creamy.
  2. Line ladyfingers at the bottom of a deep dish.
  3. Soak ladyfingers with coffee.
  4. Layer mascarpone cream on top.
  5. Continue to layer like so, as if it were lasagna.
  6. Sprinkle cocoa powder over the top.
